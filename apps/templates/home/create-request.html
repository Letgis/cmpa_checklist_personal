Billing Support

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Collect, consolidate and reconcile billing data from various sources and systems for labor and travel
• Receive Resource Unit (RU) consumption data (mainframe/server usage, help desk ticket volumes)
• Perform calculations for billing elements such as billing rates, daily rates, rebates, discounts, holdbacks, calendar month billing, Additional Resource Charges (ARC), Reduced Resource Credit (RRC), etc.
• Track purchase order balance when applicable
• Track billing against completed and approved milestones
• Prepare backup to support customer invoice
• Support to resolve billing disputes
• Create billing proforma and submit billing request in appropriate system
• Request invoice adjustments such as credits
• Prepare manual invoices from system invoices when needed
• Submit billing requests in IBM system (B&I Tool) - not applicable to Canada Market

Financial Support

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Maintain project finances in financial management appropriate systems
• Support forecasting and reconciliation, attend / support financial reviews
• Support financial issue resolution, identify need for accounting adjustments (accruals, deferrals, amortizations, etc.)
• Submit to brand finance to process, produce and analyze standard financial reports, such as claim reporting

Contract/Project Closure

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Perform close out activities defined in global process framework and appropriate tool
• Provide assistance to close out related project documents (i.e., ICAs, DOUs, and deliverables)
• Consolidate and store documents as per local regulatory requirements in the appropriate repository
• Request closure of contract/work numbers

Contract / Project Initiation

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Create / organize project repository
• Create project control book
• Setup contract tools and lists for ongoing use
• Setup contract governance
• Assist with initiation of related project documents (i.e., Inter Company Agreements (ICAs) & DOUs)

Contract Document Management

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Receive contract documentation and ensure they are stored in the authorized repository
• Maintain project or program control book, provide contract documentation retrieval support
• Provide audit support for business control reviews such as Key Controls over Operations (KCO) and Key Controls over Financial Reporting (KCFR) testing and corporate audits

Project Organization & Resourcing

This category is out of scope for NA CPS and LA CPS
In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Administer on/offboarding
• Maintenance of Workforce Member Master List (WMML)
• Maintain staffing plans and schedules (including vacation and training trackers)
• Setup contract governance
• Create and maintain Team Orientation Package
• Maintain distribution lists

Supplier Management

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Obtain brand approvals for purchase orders
• Execute purchase order creation for HW, SW and subcontractor services in compliance with brand policies. Note for some segments defined procurement practices exist limiting access to purchase order creation to only authorized roles outside of Q2C. In those segments, Q2C resources do not create or maintain purchase orders.
• Support vendor payment processes
• Track vendor purchase order balances and expiration dates
• Track, validate and/or verify vendor hours and expenses as applicable

Change Management

In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Execution of generic contract change management process
• Maintain a log of all change requests and changes
• Obtain mandatory internal approvals
• Support stakeholders on applicable actions resulting from signed changes
• Store backup documentation in support of contract change
• Request work number to be created, extended, or updated

Governance & Performance Management

This support category applies to CPS only (NA and LA)
In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Prepare for and participate in internal/external performance review meetings and retain the meeting artifacts
• Organize, receive, and store artifacts related to contract obligations for performance, such as results of client surveys
• Maintain master project/program schedule/calendar

Compliance & Controls Management

• Coordinate responses related to CRM audit and/or MSAC (Management Self Assessment Control) certification reviews as appropriate

Baseline Management

This support category applies to CPS only (NA and LA)
In-Scope Activities Required? Hours Per Month
• Create and maintain baseline records
• Ensure there is a completed Managers Checklist logged in the appropriate repository for each delivery-owned baseline record
• Store document artifacts relating to the process in the agreed repository
• Perform an annual review of records and update where required
• Validate and assign owner, responsible for providing the delivery owned resource unit count to each baseline recorded in appropriate repository
• Track and communicate baseline changes
